01 · POSITIONING

Real snacks.
Read the label twice.

Most "healthy" snacks lean on filler oils and ingredient lists you have to translate. Crackl flips that — chickpeas, real flavor, certified clean. Nothing borrowed from the lab.

Three flavors. One bag size for now, one trail-mix pouch for on-the-go. Built to live in the snack drawer at home and the gym bag on the way out.

02 · WORDMARK

Built to be read
across a grocery aisle.

The wordmark is set in Fredoka, a chunky rounded sans with friendly terminals. The dropped "e" in "Crackl" turns the missing letter into a brand signature — and shortens the syllable to a sharp, percussive bite. The period locks it down as a confident statement.

07 · DESIGN DIRECTIONS

Ten ways to wear
the same brand.

Each variation pulls a different typographic lever — vertical stacks, mega-bold, repeats, angles, stencils, photographic — while holding the same color system and wordmark. All three flavors (BBQ, Spicy, Sea Salt) shown in every trio.

08 · RODEO-INSPIRED SYSTEM

The other
direction.

Inspired by Rodeo Snacks' design DNA — cream upper portion + colored scalloped blob, mixed serif/sans typography, playful badges. The first 5 lean into the floral/scalloped energy; the last 5 strip the flowers down to cleaner geometric dividers.
