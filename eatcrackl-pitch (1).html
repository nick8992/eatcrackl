01 · POSITIONING

Real snacks.
Read the label twice.

Most "healthy" snacks lean on filler oils and ingredient lists you have to translate. Crackl flips that — chickpeas, real flavor, certified clean. Nothing borrowed from the lab.

Three flavors. One bag size for now, one trail-mix pouch for on-the-go. Built to live in the snack drawer at home and the gym bag on the way out.

02 · ORIGIN STORY

Built in a classroom.
Engineered for the snack aisle.

Crackl wasn't dreamed up in a hummus-loving home kitchen. It was engineered. Two brothers met in their university's Senior Design capstone — the most demanding real-world challenge of their undergraduate careers — and walked away with the Best Senior Design Project in the entire graduating cohort.

That experience didn't just earn them a degree. It taught them how to build something from nothing: how to identify a problem, engineer a solution, iterate relentlessly, and ship. Eighteen months later they reconnected with their professor — now a co-founder — and a new project was born. Not a mechanical system this time. A brand.

And the story keeps writing itself. For Crackl's launch, the founding team is sponsoring a Senior Design project in the very same class where it all started. Next year's engineering students will work on a real challenge connected to Crackl, and the brand's origin becomes a living, growing narrative.
